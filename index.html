About Me

For the last 6 years I have worked in the nonprofit sector. While obtaining my Master's in Pulic Administration, I worked as a Senate Aide at the Georgia State Capitol. I was hired by Sen. Nan Orrock (D-36), who brought me on as an Executive Assistant at the organization she worked for outside of public office, Women's Action for New Directions (WAND). I eventually moved on from WAND and worked as a social media content contractor for United Way of Greater Atlanta. Once my contract had ended, I began work at Living Room, a nonprofit that provides housing services for people living with HIV/AIDS. While working at Living Room, I became increasingly interested in front-end and back-end development and decided I wanted to take the leap, and change my career.
